Why did Chicago reverse the river?

When raw sewage and other pollutants were dumped in the river, they flowed into Chicago`s primary source of drinking water. As the city grew, fear of diseases spread, and officials decided to permanently reverse the river`s flow, sendingits polluted water the Mississippi River instead.
